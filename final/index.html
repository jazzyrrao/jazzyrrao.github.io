What To Drink

Orange Juice

  • A classic source of Vitamin C.
  • Refreshing.
  • Yeasty and fruity.
  • A tangy acidity.
  • Good any time of day.

Strawberry Milk

  • Light and refreshing.
  • Creamy texture.
  • Basically strawberry shortcake in a bottle.
  • Milky-strawberry.

Milktea

  • Tea-based.
  • Creamy, and silky texture.
  • A hint of floral taste.
  • Chewy tapioca pearls.

Probiotic Milk

  • A mix of citrus and vanilla.
  • Has a citrus flavor that is not similar to any other citrus fruit.
  • Yogurty and milky.
  • A sweet-yet-tart taste.
  • Rich in good bacteria.
